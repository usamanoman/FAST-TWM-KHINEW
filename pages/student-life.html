FACILITIES

The National University has incorporated computer and engineering labs into its campus that contain latest computers and sophisticated hardware that is constantly refined to reflect latest changes in the technology. The staff is friendly and always eager to help and guide students. The libraries are well-equipped with all the books, journals and magazines etc to enlighten students. Due to the tireless efforts of NU staff, there is now an online library catalogue that provides electronic versions of books and journals etc for the students to download. The library is spacious and has an environment that encourages and supports the learning of the students.

 

Hostels

 
Separate hostels for boys and girls are also there at Gulshan-e-Iqbal and Karimabad respectively in main Karachi city. University provided tranport services are also available for the hostel students.
 

Transport

 
The University arranges buses to pick students from selected points in different localities of Karachi.Arranged transport is also available for those studying at the City Campus. The transport facility is available on self-finance basis.

Placement & Industry Liaison Office

 
These is a dedicated Placement & Industry Liaison Office at the campus which develops and maintains strong relations with the corporate world. It facilitates students and graduates in getting top quality internships and jobs. Top students are hired just before graduation by top companies and the job placement rate of graduates is 100%.
