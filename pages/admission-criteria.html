HOW TO APPLY

Fill online form at the website mentioned above. After filling the form the candidate will be issued an ARN (application reference number), the candidate has to then himself visit the City campus with his ARN and the following set of documents.

  • (Original & Copy): Matriculation marks sheet OR O-level certificates
  • Intermediate Part-I marks sheet OR A-level certificates.
  • Admit Card of 2nd year Or Statement of Entry for A-Level.
  • Copy of CNIC or FORM-B.
  • 3 Photographs
  • Test registration fee. Rs. 1000 for one test
