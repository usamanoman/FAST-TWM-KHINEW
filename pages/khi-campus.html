WELCOME TO FAST

The Campus life at NUCES is productive and energetic. The campus provides excellent environment for learning and experimentation, students can utilize all the facilities in the campus as they like. Students can always go to library for information hunt, or they can put their ideas into practice by working in the well-equipped laboratories.

Students are often found working on their laptops on the lush green grounds on NUCES while enjoying a cup of tea. Group studies and peer-to-peer learning is also a common sight throughout the campus.

However, life at NUCES is not all about studying, students can go to Activity block and enjoy a number of indoor games. Also, a gymnasium has been setup in the activity block for students to keep their fitness. Apart from indoor games, the campus also has grounds for almost all the games, grounds for futsal, cricket, volley ball, and basket ball are within the premises of the Campus.

Student bodies also periodically organize e-gaming competitions at all Karachi level to bring out the best of the gaming community of Karachi. These events attract a lot of attention not only in Campus but also in the metropolis.
